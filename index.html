What is Ply

Ply is our first project, currently in development. We have a ravine we want to cross. In this example, we could play ”joy” to make a tree grow from a grass to take us to the other side, or we could also play “sadness”, so that it starts to rain, a river forms and we can swim across. Each player forms his experience/music/environment with his own emotions/decisions.
